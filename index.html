ABOUT

the game

inspired by...

Fish Beat Down (actual game title to be decided by the community) is a web GL based game for raining down blow after blow on your opponent’s fish. There’s combos, special moves, special weapons, and blood-curdling finishes. Fishes about to get beat!

You must own a fish to play!

Your NFT is your character, and each
of the 10,000 Dojo Fish are playable.

The game is peer-to-peer, or you can just
play the AI (who is one very slimy fish).

Shortly after mint, your NFT will grant you access to the training dojo, where you can level-up and prep your fish for gameplay.

Initial holders and community members will be invited to beta-test the game and provide input.

Your NFT isn’t like other NFTS, it has special gaming attributes that increase its in-game performance — the more hours and wins logged, the more badass your fish becomes.

This is just a small preview of what’s to come, we’ve assembled an elite team on the level of Aaron Blaise to handle every aspect of this game.

ABOUT

Few artists are as iconic as Academy Award nominated animation Director Aaron Blaise.

He was part of Walt Disney’s Feature Animation team and over 21 years worked on some of the world's most popular feature films and directed the feature film “Brother Bear” in 2003 which was nominated for 22 awards and won 2.

He’s the animator behind countless original Disney characters.

  . Beast from Beauty and the Beast
   . Jasmine & Rajah from Aladdin
   . Young Nala in The Lion King
   . Pocahontas in Pocahontas
   . Yao and the Ancestors in Mulan
